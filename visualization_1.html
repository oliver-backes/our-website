The Ability of Social Media to Legitimize Fake News

Comparing the ability of Social Media to spread Fake News to the Traditional Press

This study will use data from mediacloud.org to track the spread of false news stories before and after being endorsed by a sample of Social Media "Influencers"

Possible Visualations may include:

  • timelines tracking the lifecycle of a story based on number of mentions in traditional media.
  • circles of expanding or contracting size demonstrating influence before and after social media endorsement.
