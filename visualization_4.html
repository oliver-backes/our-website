Visualization 4: Global map of Fake News in the media

Using data from medicloud.org, this page will visualize a heat map which highlights the frequency of the phrase 'fake news' in that country's media. We will also try to add a sliding timescale to visualize the monthly changes in use of fake news dating back to June 2016.
