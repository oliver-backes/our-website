Fake News on Facebook

Why do Facebook pages publish fake news? Are users more likely to engage with false stories than with accurate stories?

This comparative analysis of false and accurate stories on nine political Facebook pages examines which sort of stories achieve higher user engagement, as quantified by likes, comments, and shares (data courtesy of Buzzfeed).
